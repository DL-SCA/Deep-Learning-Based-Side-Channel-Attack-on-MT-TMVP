## Conclusions and Recommendations


### Strong Leakage Detected

The TVLA analysis reveals significant information leakage in the power traces:

1. **Traces contain exploitable information** - The t-test shows statistically significant 
   differences between trace groups, indicating data-dependent power consumption.

2. **Multiple leakage sources** - Both input and output values appear to influence power consumption,
   providing multiple attack vectors.

   should be able to learn the relationship between traces and secret key coefficients.
